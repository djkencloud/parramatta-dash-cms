Parramatta is transforming.

Parramatta is changing fast. More jobs, more green spaces, more cultural facilities, more people. This site explores the data on Parramatta today - towards understanding the city of Parramatta tomorrow.
We’re exploring what kinds of information is out there on Parramatta as a city in transition. Over time, we want the site evolving to become a citizen’s dashboard of Parramatta. Find out what is planned for the area, how the city has evolved over time, and what’s trending in the city right now.
